What is this app?

This app will allow you to track your medication use and remind you when you should take your medication.

How do I use it

Press the plus button in the home tab. You can then enter the necessary info.

The app will store this data with your login, so you can log in anywhere and access it

Where is the data stored?

We store the information serverside. This means it is accessible anywhere at anytime. You can log into the app from any phone, and see your information.
